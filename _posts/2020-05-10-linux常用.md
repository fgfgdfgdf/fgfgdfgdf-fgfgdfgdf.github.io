### centos修改环境变量
```shell
vi /etc/profile

# 添加内容:
JAVA_HOME=/java/java8/jdk

PATH=$JAVA_HOME/bin:$PATH
CLASSPATH=.:$JAVA_HOME/lib/dt.jar:$JAVA_HOME/lib/tools.jar
export JAVA_HOME 
export PATH
export CLASSPATH

```
